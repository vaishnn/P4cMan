Tired of the command line?

Managing Python projects can mean juggling endless `pip` commands, `requirements.txt` files, and hidden dependency conflicts. PacMan brings clarity and control into a single, visual workspace.

Everything You Need to Manage Your Projects

Effortless Package Control

Search, install, update, and remove packages from PyPI with a simple, intuitive interface. Manage specific versions and handle dependencies automatically.

Full Environment Management

Create, delete, and switch between virtual environments seamlessly. Generate `requirements.txt` files to ensure your builds are always reproducible.

Proactive Security Scanning

Automatically scan your installed packages for known security vulnerabilities and get clear, actionable advice on how to resolve them.

Visualize Your Workflow

Untangle Complex Dependencies

Stop guessing how your packages are connected. PacMan generates an interactive dependency graph, allowing you to visually trace relationships, identify potential conflicts, and understand your project's architecture at a glance.

Make Data-Driven Security Decisions

Don't just manage packages; manage risk. Our security dashboard presents vulnerability data in a clear, organized way, helping you prioritize updates and maintain a secure, professional development environment.
